biography

Miloš Krunić

My affinity for graphic design can be traced back to my childhood. Growing up in a household where my father worked as a photographer, I was constantly surrounded by images - landscapes, portraits, moments frozen in time. That early exposure planted a deep appreciation for visual storytelling and a desire to create beauty in all its forms. As I got older, I found that design gave me the tools to express that instinct in a modern, digital language. I was especially drawn to minimalism, bold symbolism, and color combinations that burn themselves into your memory the moment you see them.
Just as the photographs of my childhood shaped the way I see the world, those early impressions continue to echo through my work today.
